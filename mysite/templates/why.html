What is QuickShift and what does it do?

QuickShift is a one-stop shop for everything you need for creating a schedule.

Manages Availability

QuickShift provides a form to manage the staff's availability in a way that is both user-friendly and readable. QuickShift stores the spreadsheet in the cloud, where it automatically saves itself. Here, managers can share this form with a simple link with anyone in the world.

Manages Needed Staff

QuickShift provides a form to manage the needed staff for each shift simply and intuitively. This form is stored in the cloud and saved automatically. Managers can share this sheet with a simple link with anyone in the world.

Generates the Schedule

QuickShift pulls from both the availability form and the needed staff sheet and automagically creates the schedule for the next week within seconds. It also provides an easy download of the schedule in a readable format.

Requests Off

With QuickShift, any employees can request off whenever and wherever they want. This simplicity makes it easier for both staff and managers by creating a smoother request process. QuickShift stores all requests in a spreadsheet where managers can nullify an appeal if they deem it necessary. QuickShift can also read these requests and avoid scheduling employees if requested off. However, if there is not ample staff, QuickShift will override requests.

The Current Schedule QuickLink

Managers can upload the current schedule into QuickShift, where the staff can view it. This feature allows employees can bookmark this link and check it to view the schedule.

QuickSend

Once the managers upload the schedule, QuickShift gives them the option to QuickSend (email) the schedule to all staff in the database with a custom message.
